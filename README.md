# FUTURE_DS_02
Telco Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview:

This project presents an interactive Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze customer retention patterns in a telecommunications company. The dashboard helps identify the key factors contributing to customer churn and provides actionable insights to improve customer retention strategies.

Objectives:
Analyze customer churn behavior.
Identify high-risk customer segments.
Understand the impact of contract type, payment method, internet service, tenure, and monthly charges on churn.
Support data-driven decision-making for reducing customer attrition.

Key Metrics:
Total Customers: 7,043
Churn Customers: 2,000
Churn Rate: 26.54%

Dashboard Insights:
Internet Service Analysis
Customers using Fiber Optic services exhibit the highest churn rate.
DSL customers show moderate churn levels.
Customers without internet service have the lowest churn.

Contract Type Analysis:
Month-to-Month contracts account for the majority of churned customers.
Customers with One-Year and Two-Year contracts demonstrate significantly better retention.

Payment Method Analysis:
Customers paying through Electronic Check show the highest churn levels.
Automated payment methods such as bank transfer and credit card have lower churn rates.

Tenure Analysis:
New customers with shorter tenure periods (<15 months) are more likely to churn.
Churn decreases as customer tenure increases.

Monthly Charges Analysis:
Customers with higher monthly charges (> $60) tend to churn more frequently.
Price sensitivity appears to be a major factor affecting retention.

Senior Citizen Analysis:
Non-senior customers represent the majority of churned customers.
Senior citizens contribute a smaller portion of total churn.

Gender Analysis:
Churn distribution between male and female customers is relatively balanced, indicating gender has minimal impact on churn behavior.

Tools & Technologies:
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Data Modeling
Data Visualization

Business Recommendations:
Introduce retention offers for customers on month-to-month contracts.
Improve customer experience for Fiber Optic users.
Encourage customers to adopt automated payment methods.
Focus retention campaigns on new customers during their first year.
Review pricing strategies for customers with high monthly charges.

Outcome:
The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify churn drivers, monitor customer retention trends, and develop targeted strategies to reduce customer attrition and improve customer lifetime value.

Skills Demonstrated: Power BI, Data Cleaning, Data Modeling, DAX Measures, KPI Development, Dashboard Design, Customer Analytics, Business Intelligence, Data Visualization.
